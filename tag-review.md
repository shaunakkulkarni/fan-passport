## Tag Dimensions

Each club has 4 new intangible tags (v2) plus existing v1 tags. The 4 new dimensions are what the v2 quiz matches against:

### 1. `stability`
How the club is run institutionally.
- **stable** — Long-term sporting direction, low managerial turnover, institutional patience
- **chaotic** — Frequent manager changes, owner drama, transfer sagas, passionate instability
- **rebuilding** — Currently in a turnaround phase, new ownership or philosophy
- **win-now** — Aggressive spending, short-term focus, trophy-or-bust mentality

### 2. `fan-culture`
The dominant supporter culture at the club.
- **ultras** — Intense supporter culture, tifosi/ultras groups, politically engaged fans
- **family** — Modern, family-friendly matchday experience
- **intellectual** — Tactical/analytical fanbase, appreciate system and philosophy
- **rebel** — Politically charged, anti-establishment, protest culture

### 3. `ambition`
What the club prioritizes.
- **trophy-driven** — Club expects to win titles every year; fans demand silverware
- **identity-driven** — Club prioritizes philosophy/culture over results; unique identity is the product
- **smart-overachiever** — Data-driven, intelligent recruitment, outperforming budget
- **romantic** — History, passion, connection to place; results are secondary to the romance

### 4. `narrative**
The club's overarching story.
- **underdog** — Punching above weight, smaller club achieving beyond expectations
- **dynasty** — Historic or current dominant force; winning is expected
- **cursed** — Long-suffering, loyal through decades of heartbreak
- **project** — Building something new, young talent, ascending trajectory
